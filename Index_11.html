I have been the hollow stone beside still water,
I have been the fog that swallows its own name.
The wound does not ask permission to bloom
and I have learned to call the ache my flame.

The Day I Disappeared

The funny thing about it is, it didn't happen fast,
Nobody noticed as I slipped away into the past.
I was still there, still walking, still breathing every day,
But the real me, the authentic me, had slowly slipped away.

Buried Alive

I'm buried here alive now and I'm running out of air,
And all I have around me is the crushing, dark despair.
The suffocation deepens with each breath that I do take,
And I wonder how much longer that my soul can ever break.

The Void Calls My Name

The world keeps getting smaller and the void keeps getting wide,
There's less of me to hold onto and more space to hide.
Each person who abandoned me has left me closer still,
To giving in completely to the void's own gentle will.

Alone in a Crowded Room

I try so hard to matter, to be seen and to be known,
But the harder that I try, the more I'm left alone.
It's like I'm screaming silently and nobody can hear,
That I need help, that I'm dying, that I'm trapped in all this fear.
